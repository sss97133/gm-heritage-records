Frequently Asked Questions

What vehicles are eligible for GM records searches?
We can search records for any General Motors vehicle, including Chevrolet, Cadillac, Buick, Oldsmobile, Pontiac, GMC, Saturn, and Hummer. Records availability varies by model year and brand.
How far back do GM service records go?
GM has maintained comprehensive records since the 1950s, with more limited records available for earlier vehicles. The most complete records are typically available for vehicles manufactured after 1965.
What information will I receive?
Our standard package includes all available service records, warranty information, original build specifications, and dealer maintenance history. The exact documents vary based on what GM has available for your specific vehicle.
How long does it take to receive the records?
Most record requests are processed within 15 business days. Complex requests or very rare vehicles may take longer to research.
Do I need the original VIN to request records?
Yes, a VIN or chassis number is required. For pre-1981 vehicles that don't have a 17-digit VIN, the shorter chassis number is acceptable.
